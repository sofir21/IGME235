Project 3: Dino-digger

High Concept:
"You're an archeologist, and must dig around the bones while making sure to not break it! Locate randomly-placed hidden bones by clicking on the stones around them, and find the bones with help from clues about the number of neighbouring bones. Since the bones are delicate, if you accidentally click on them the bone will break and make the entire fossil worthless. This is a fun take on minesweeper, but with a concept that can seem interesting to kids."

Genre:
Puzzle game.

Platform:
Web

Story:
Like stated in the concept, the premise is you're an archeologist.

Aesthetic:
I'm trying to go for the aesthetic of regular flash games you'd play online as a 2000s kid. Hopefully, most of the sprites will be drawn.

Gameplay:
The only controls used in this game will be the mouse button and click, with the left click they'll dig up what they believe are stones, and with the right click they will mark what they believe are possible bone blocks.
