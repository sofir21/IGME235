About Me

    Hey there! I'm a 3rd year Game Design and Development student in Rochester Institute of Technology. I was born in Nicaragua, but have been living in Rochester since I started school three years ago. Before coming to RIT, I had never previously done any coding in my life, but in the past few years I have learned a lot thanks to classes and personal projects! I'm interested in working in a place where I feel challenged and have the potential to learn new things and put them in practice.

    When I'm not doing work, I tend to spend my time drawing (I'm getting a minor in 2D Art) and playing video games! Some of my favorite games to play in my free time are: Legend of Zelda (any in the series), League of Legends, Minecraft, and D&D!
