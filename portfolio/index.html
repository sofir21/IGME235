Projects

Changeling VR Hub Level

Changeling is a narrative mystery virtual reality experience. The previous design of the hub world was narratively unfitting and uninteresting from a gameplay perspective-- as the level designer and art lead, I was requested to redesign the level the ground up in a month for a game demonstration.

Technologies used: Unreal Engine, Maya, Photoshop

  • Developed new design for the level that allowed for more intuitive exploration and was thematically fitting with the main character.
  • Integrated pre-existing game mechanics into the new level to create an engaging experience with limited development time.
  • Accelerated development process by assisting 3D team by creating models and texturing their work.
  • Collaborated with development team to ensure assets were implemented properly and the design was properly translated into the product.
  • Produced a playable level within the time constraint provided.

10 Weeks

10 Weeks is a satirical, comedic visual novel created in RenPy. It pokes fun at visual novel tropes, RIT culture and gamer culture. The game was a week-and-a-half long personal project, created with the intention of experiencing one-person game development and to learn a new coding language. Since it was mostly a learning experience (and I worked on in the middle of a semester) it was left unfinished, but there is a working demo of the first part of the game.

Technologies used: RenPy, Photoshop, Python

  • Created story line and designed game mechanics.
  • Produced concepts and illustrated them into sprites for the game.
  • Designed UI that fit the aesthetic and was fully functional.
  • Self-taught Python and programmed a demo.

SLIME

SLIME is a 2D platformer created in MonoGame by a four-person team called 1HP Slimes. You play as a slime stuck in the sewers trying to escape. Our goal with the game was to create a platformer that plays with the fact that you are a slime; most of the puzzles in the game involve sticking to the walls or ceiling and unsticking to avoid enemies and reach the end of the level.

Technologies used: Monogame, C#

  • Coordinated work times for the team and delegated work to every member to streamline the process of development.
  • Conceptualized mechanics and visual style of the game to make it fit the theme we established.
  • Implemented enemy movement, player movement, and collisions.
  • Assisted team members who felt overwhelmed by their workload or were unable to complete their tasks.
